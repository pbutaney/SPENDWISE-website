SpendWise Showcase
Introduction

SpendWise is designed to help users manage travel budgets, expenses, and savings for solo and group trips. Its goal is to simplify financial tracking and planning while providing insights through AI-driven recommendations.

Design & Architecture

Architecture: MVVM pattern for modularity and maintainability.

Design Patterns: Observer pattern for real-time dashboard updates.
